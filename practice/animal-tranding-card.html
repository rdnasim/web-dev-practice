Butterflies

There are about 24,000 species of butterflies.

  • Scientific Name: Rhopalocera
  • Average Length: 8 to 12 inches
  • Average Weight: 0.03 to 3 grams
  • Life Span: Up to 12 months

Butterflies are considered by many to be the
most beautiful and interesting of insects.
Many people watch and collect butterflies
as a hobby. One of the most distinguishing
features of butterflies is their bright
and colorful wings of many different patterns.
